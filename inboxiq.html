Revolutionize Your Email Experience with AI-Powered Insights

About InboxIQ

InboxIQ is an innovative Chrome extension designed to transform the way you interact with your emails. By harnessing the power of artificial intelligence, InboxIQ provides smart summaries and facilitates intelligent conversations about your emails, all within your browser.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Email Summaries: Get concise summaries of lengthy emails at a glance.
  • Intelligent Conversations: Engage in meaningful dialogues about your emails with our AI assistant.
  • Privacy-First Approach: Your data remains in your control, processed using your own AI service API keys.
  • Seamless Integration: Works directly within your Gmail interface for a smooth user experience.

How It Works

InboxIQ integrates seamlessly with your Gmail account. When you select an email, our extension processes the content locally in your browser. For generating summaries and responses, the data is sent to third-party AI services using your provided API key. This ensures that you have full control over which service processes your data.

Privacy and Security

At InboxIQ, we prioritize your privacy and data security. We do not store or access your email data on our servers. All processing is done either locally in your browser or through secure connections to the AI service of your choice. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.
