Why use it?

InboxIQ is a powerful Google Chrome Extension designed to save you time and streamline your email workflow. It excels at summarizing long emails or complex threads, giving you the key points at a glance. Need more information? You can ask follow-up questions or make general queries about the content, and InboxIQ will provide concise, relevant answers. By quickly getting you up to speed with the key details in the emails, this extension significantly reduces the time you spend managing your inbox. Whether you're catching up after time away or dealing with information-heavy emails, InboxIQ helps you process information faster and more efficiently, making it an invaluable tool for professionals and busy individuals alike.
