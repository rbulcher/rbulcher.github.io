Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Introduction

Welcome to InboxIQ. This privacy policy outlines our commitment to protecting your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Process

InboxIQ accesses and processes the following information:

  • Email content from your Gmail account
  • Email thread IDs

This information is processed entirely within your browser and is sent directly to OpenAI for generating summaries. We do not store, view, or process your email data on any servers.

How We Use Information

The information accessed by InboxIQ is used solely for providing you with email summaries. We act as a direct interface between your browser and OpenAI, without any intermediary storage or processing.

Data Transmission to Third Parties

InboxIQ sends email data directly to OpenAI to generate summaries. We require users to provide their own OpenAI API key, ensuring that:

  • You have direct control over the AI service processing your data
  • The processing adheres to OpenAI's privacy policy
  • We never act as a middleman in data transmission

We strongly recommend reviewing OpenAI's privacy policy to understand how they handle your data when you use your API key.

Data Storage

InboxIQ uses Chrome's local storage to save conversation history, email summaries, and settings. This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to or stored on our servers. Your OpenAI API key is also stored securely on your device.

Third-Party Services

InboxIQ uses the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth 2.0 for Gmail authentication
  • OpenAI for generating email summaries
  • Stripe for handling Pro version subscriptions (if applicable)

Please be aware of the respective privacy policies of these services.

Pro Version Subscription

If you subscribe to the Pro version of InboxIQ, an account is created for you with Stripe, a world-renowned secure payment processor. Stripe handles all authentication and payment processing. We do not store or have access to your payment information.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. All communication with OpenAI is done using secure HTTPS connections. Your OpenAI API key is stored securely in Chrome's local storage and is only used to make direct requests to OpenAI.

User Control

You have full control over your data. You can delete all stored conversations and summaries at any time using the 'Delete All' function in the extension. You control which AI service processes your data by providing your own OpenAI API key.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the extension.
